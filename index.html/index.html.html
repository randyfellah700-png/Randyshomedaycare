About Us

At Randy's Home Daycare, we provide a warm, loving, and nurturing environment for children aged 6 months to 5 years. Located in Kiganjo, Thika, we pride ourselves on offering quality care where every child feels safe, valued, and supported.

Our daycare emphasizes personal attention, healthy meals, and fun learning through play. We ensure that each child’s emotional, social, and physical growth flourishes under professional care.

Tuition & Fees

  • 6 months – 1 year: Ksh 150 per day
  • 1 year and above: Ksh 100 per day
  • Kenyan long-term: Ksh 7,000 per month
  • Gulf long-term: Ksh 10,000 per month

Payments can be made weekly or monthly through M-PESA or cash.
